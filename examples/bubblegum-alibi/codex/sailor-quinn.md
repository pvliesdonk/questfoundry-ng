title: Sailor Quinn, Dance Captain
---

Juno's best friend and quiet, unspoken crush — the good-hearted dance captain whose scholarship rode entirely on tonight's showcase. She arrives in a sequined dance-off costume, one sleeve torn, glitter caught bright in her hair like a girl who was, hours ago, invincible. On the surface she is warm and steady, the sort you'd trust to catch you mid-lift. But tonight something has cracked the steadiness: her hands shake, and her answers arrive a beat too late, as if she's checking each one against a story she's afraid to tell. Everyone loves Sailor. That's the trouble with loving Sailor. The nicest girl in the room is also the one with the most to lose before dawn — and the almanac notes, without comment, that those two things are seldom unrelated.
