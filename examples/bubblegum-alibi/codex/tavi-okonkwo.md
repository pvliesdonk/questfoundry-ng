title: Tavi Okonkwo, at the Decks
---

The lock-in's DJ and Juno's partner in the great yearbook conspiracy — loyal, funny, and the person who cut the lights for the dance finale. Spot them by the oversized headphones, the glow tape striping their sneakers, and the laptop bag they will not, under any circumstance, set down; cornered, they clamp it to their side like a life vest in open water. Tavi is easy company, quick with a joke, quicker with a beat drop. But mention the blackout and watch the flinch cross their face, small and involuntary, gone before you're sure you saw it. They cut the lights for the finale. That much everyone agrees on. Whether the darkness lasted exactly as long as they say — well, the almanac only records that Tavi hums when nervous, and hasn't stopped humming since the music died.
