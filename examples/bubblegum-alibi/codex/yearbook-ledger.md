title: Juno's Secret Ledger
---

Not the glossy yearbook the school will publish, but its shadow twin: a hidden notebook where Juno records what the captions can't say. Every buried shame, every quiet ruin, every motive folded small and pressed flat between pages — the whole room's private weather, mapped in one editor's cramped hand. She keeps it tucked against her ribs beneath her cardigan, close as a second heartbeat, because whoever holds the ledger holds what the principal will learn come dawn. It is the most dangerous object in a chained room full of dangerous people, and Juno knows it. She swore she'd never turn it against a soul. The almanac makes no promises on her behalf. It only observes that a book of everyone's secrets is a very useful thing on the night one of those secrets stops being buried — and starts, instead, to bleed.
