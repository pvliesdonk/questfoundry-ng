The stone hallway caught the storm’s cold breath, spilling it across the worn flagstones. Jordan slipped past the deputy who had just pressed the badge against their shoulder, the metal biting into the fabric of the coat. The sheriff’s shout rang through the great room, but the investigator moved behind a column and disappeared into the narrow western passage. Their notebook remained clutched to their chest, the fresh accusation against Marta Valen still sharp in their mind.

The deputy’s orders fell short as Jordan vanished into shadow, the echo of boots fading behind the thick timber doors. They paused in the dim alcove, feeling the stone’s chill against the back of the neck, and counted the seconds of silence. The lodge’s authority thinned with each step taken away from the hearth, and the investigator resolved to pursue the claim that Marta’s alibi was a fabrication without the sheriff’s eye upon them.

A faint rustle announced another presence. Simon Kade stepped from the gloom, the immaculate uniform barely stirring in the drafts. He raised a mug, the porcelain cool in his grip, and spoke in a low tone. “There is a passage that skirts the main hall,” he said. He placed a gloved hand on a tapestry hung low on the wall, the fabric shifting to reveal a narrow seam in the stone. The hidden door was barely wide enough for a single figure, its outline whispered in the torchlight.

Jordan accepted the offered lead, slipping a foot through the opening and feeling the cold air curl around their boots. The corridor beyond narrowed, the walls slick with moisture, and the investigator’s breath formed brief clouds that vanished as quickly as the storm outside battered the shutters. Each turn was noted in the mind, a map of escape routes that kept the inquiry alive beyond the sheriff’s reach. The accusation against Marta burned still, a silent ember driving the steps forward.

The last echo of Simon’s footsteps faded, leaving only the soft scrape of stone under Jordan’s soles. The lodge’s hidden veins stretched ahead, promising secret evidence and a chance to untangle the tangled alibi without the law’s interference.
