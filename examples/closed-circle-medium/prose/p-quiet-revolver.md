The revolver lay idle on the desk, the smudged prints a reminder of recent handling, yet Jordan kept their hand off the weapon. They slipped the notebook back into the coat pocket and turned their gaze toward Marta, who hovered near the brass rail, her eyes scanning the stone walls. Jordan chose to preserve the hostess’s reputation, refraining from pressing further, allowing the guest’s narrative to hold.
