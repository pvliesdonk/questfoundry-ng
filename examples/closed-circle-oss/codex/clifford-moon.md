title: Clifford Moon – Traveling Salesman
---

Clifford Moon, a traveling salesman whose smile never quite reaches his eyes, cuts a polished figure wherever the dusty roads lead. His hair is slicked back into a glossy sheen, and he favors a flashy suit that flashes brighter than the sun‑baked storefronts he hauls wares into. He speaks with a charm that feels rehearsed, slipping away from direct answers as if each word were a coin he could toss. Rumours coil around his past—shadows of debts unpaid and favors owed—yet no record confirms his transgressions. In a town that measures men by their hands and the weight of their promises, Clifford remains an enigma wrapped in silk.
