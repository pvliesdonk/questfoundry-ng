title: Farmer Union – Cooperative of Local Growers
---

The Farmer Union is a cooperative of the town’s tillers, bound together by a shared wariness of outside influence. Its meetings convene under the shade of weather‑worn barns, where petitions are inked in blood‑thick resolve and banners bearing a simple plow are raised. Members speak in low tones, their voices steeped in the earth they coax from stubborn soil, demanding respect for their livelihoods against encroaching authority. Though they lack the polished trappings of the town’s elite, their numbers and perseverance give them a quiet strength that can shift the balance of power when the harvest moon rises. In the fields, the Union’s resolve grows as stubbornly as the crops they tend.
