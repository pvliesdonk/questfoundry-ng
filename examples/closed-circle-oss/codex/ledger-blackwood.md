title: Blackwood Ledger – Ledger of Debts and Blackmail
---

The Blackwood Ledger rests in a hidden safe, its iron clasp concealed behind rusted machinery deep within the Old Mill’s forgotten chambers. Bound in weathered leather, the tome lists debts, favors, and blackmail in a script as precise as a clock’s gear. Its presence is known to few, and even fewer have glimpsed its pages, wary of the power such knowledge bestows. The safe’s location, cloaked behind twisted metal and the smell of oil, ensures that only the most determined—or the most desperate—might retrieve it. In a town where reputation is currency, the ledger stands as a silent ledger of shadows, waiting for a hand brave enough to turn its pages.
