title: Lucy Frost – Bartender and Former Boxer
---

Lucy Frost tends the bar with the same hard grip she once used in the ring. Muscular and bald, she meets each newcomer’s stare with a stone‑cold glare that could chill steel. A fresh, dark scar mars her right palm—a reminder of a recent blaze that left more than just flesh smoldering. Blunt to a fault and fiercely protective of her patrons, she guards the tavern’s back door as if it were the final ring in a fight. Her past as a boxer is etched into every movement, each swing of a glass a reminder of a disciplined body forced to stop beating prematurely. In the town’s dim corners, Lucy is the unyielding wall against the night’s creeping doubts.
