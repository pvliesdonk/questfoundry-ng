title: Marjorie Lynch – Diner Owner
---

Marjorie Lynch runs the local diner with a round, flour‑dusted apron that seems to swallow the kitchen’s heat whole. Friendly eyes and a nosy disposition make her the unofficial conduit of gossip, threading rumors through the clatter of plates and the hiss of coffee machines. Her matriarchal presence commands the morning rush, and she greets each traveler like a long‑lost kin. Though the town’s secrets swirl around her like steam, Marjorie rarely lets a whisper escape unexamined, cataloguing every half‑truth as if she were binding them into a ledger of communal memory. In a place where words feed as much as meals, she stands at the crossroads of truth and rumor.
