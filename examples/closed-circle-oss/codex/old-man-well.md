title: Silas "Old Man" Well – Clockmaker
---

Silas 'Old Man' Well shuffles through the market square, stooped and wiry, his eyes reflecting the tick of countless gears he’s tended. As the town’s ancient clockmaker, he knows the hidden passages that lace beneath the cobblestones, routes whispered only to those who listen to the subtle cadence of time. Cryptic in speech yet patient in counsel, he offers a single maxim to those who seek his aid: 'Force shatters peace.' His workshop is a sanctuary of brass and pendulums, the air thick with oil and the scent of rusted metal. In a world where moments are measured, Old Man Well guards the seconds that might otherwise slip into darkness.
