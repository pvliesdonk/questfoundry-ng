title: Old Mill – Abandoned River Mill
---

The Old Mill looms on the river’s bend, its stone ribs cracked by time and its wheel long stilled. Abandoned, it has become the whispered haunt of those seeking refuge or hidden purpose, rumors painting it as a hideout for the desperate. A rusted iron latch secures the stone hatch that guards a lower passage, its hinges groaning with each wind that rattles the broken windows. The water’s perpetual murmur flows around its foundation, masking any footfall that might echo within. In the night, lantern light flickers across the soot‑blackened walls, casting shadows that dance like conspiratorial figures. The mill remains a silent sentinel, holding secrets in its mortar and the darkness beneath.
