title: Thea Swan – New Nurse
---

Thea Swan arrived with a bob haircut tucked beneath a crisp medical coat, her quiet vigilance evident in every measured step. As the town’s newest nurse, she moves through wards with a barely audible breath, eyes flickering over wounds as if scanning for hidden fault lines. Rumors, like ill‑kept patients, whisper that she may be fleeing a scandal, though no concrete accusation has surfaced. Her composure remains unshaken, balancing the dual demands of care and self‑preservation. In the hush of hospital corridors, Thea embodies the fragile steadiness of a heartbeat held at the brink, trusting that any revelation will be met with the same measured calm she extends to others.
