title: The Oversized Antique Harpoon
---

The Oversized Antique Harpoon is a monumentally ill-conceived piece of nautical decor that hangs in the study with an aura of precarious menace. Forged from heavy, rusted iron and splintering maritime wood, this ridiculous weapon looks entirely absurd mounted above a delicate mahogany writing desk. Its presence in a landlocked aristocratic manor is an architectural non sequitur of the highest order. The mounting brackets securing the harpoon to the wall are thoroughly inadequate for its staggering weight, resulting in a terrifying tendency for the weapon to unexpectedly detach from the plaster. It exists in a permanent state of gravitational rebellion, threatening to plummet downward at the slightest atmospheric vibration or heavily closed door. The sheer heft of the object guarantees that when it inevitably surrenders to gravity, the resulting impact upon the floorboards—or any unfortunate furnishings situated below—will be both sudden and catastrophically forceful.
