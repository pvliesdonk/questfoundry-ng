title: Arthur's Encrypted Ledger
---

Arthur’s Encrypted Ledger is a formidable tome of financial documentation, tightly bound in thick, heavily aged leather. This formidable casing is notoriously combustible, designed to emit a singularly foul, acrid odor if ever subjected to the intense temperatures of a standard residential hearth. The interior leaves are composed of an excessively thick, unyielding vellum that deliberately resists the frantic turning of pages. This infuriating tactile friction forces the reader to slow down and meticulously emphasize each catastrophic entry recorded within its margins. Its physical density is staggering, granting the book a brick-like quality that makes it less of a standard ledger and more of a blunt instrument of fiscal terror. The volume's sheer structural rigidity allows it to function effortlessly as a load-bearing architectural support, should one require a desperately firm foundation to stabilize uneven domestic furnishings.
