The meticulously cultivated veneer of high-society etiquette dissolves entirely, replaced by an aggressive territoriality that threatens the structural integrity of the evening's carefully planned schedule.

He gestures with impeccable posture toward the adjacent study, reminding the deteriorating company of the paramount necessity to respect the slumber of the gentleman ostensibly resting within the armchair. His entreaties, framed with flawless syntax and referencing the most foundational tenets of hospitable consideration, are met with an appalling level of insubordination.
