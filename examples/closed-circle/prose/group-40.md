In a calibrated response to Munroe's aggressively direct ocular focus, Beaumont discovers a sudden, consuming academic interest in the parlor's expansive Persian rug. The inspector’s glare possesses a penetrative quality that threatens to breach the acceptable boundaries of hostly composure, presenting a profound challenge to domestic sovereignty. To meet such an uncivilized stare with his own gaze would constitute a tacit endorsement of her combative posture, which stands as an unacceptable concession in the rigid theater of parlor diplomacy. Therefore, he rigidly inclines his chin downward, directing his unblinking attention toward the intricate geometric medallions woven into the antique floor covering.

He studies the precise interlocking threads of carmine and indigo with the solemn dedication of an antiquities appraiser, steadfastly refusing to acknowledge the severe, demanding presence currently occupying the adjacent space. This tactical deployment of selective blindness serves an indispensable dual purpose. It firmly denies the inspector the social friction she requires to fuel her highly inappropriate forensic momentum, while simultaneously allowing Beaumont to meticulously inventory the complex floral borders for any stray droplets of arterial fluid that might have eluded his earlier, highly irregular sanitation efforts involving the heavy velvet drapery. The preservation of an untarnished aesthetic environment remains a critical obligation, even as the inspector actively projects an aura of unrefined hostility across the threshold.

By granting the floor covering his absolute and undivided attention, Beaumont constructs an impenetrable social barrier, utilizing the strict application of etiquette as a defensive fortification against external scrutiny. The interaction degrades into a prolonged test of decorum, one he fully intends to master through the sheer, unwavering observance of inanimate textiles. Nevertheless, the perpetual obligations of his position soon present a highly acceptable pretext for executing a physical withdrawal from this severely unsatisfactory configuration.
