Settled securely upon the fluffed pillows of the second-floor guest chamber, Sylvia mounts a spirited defense of her own ironclad constitution. Despite the decidedly verdant pallor of her complexion and the intermittent necessity to tightly compress her midsection, she firmly rejects any suggestion of biological frailty or malicious tampering. Her current indisposition is characterized exclusively as a profound aesthetic objection to the host's purchasing habits. She delivers a rigorous, albeit halting, lecture concerning the severe social penalties associated with serving economy-grade foliage to guests of discerning pedigree. To admit she is genuinely unwell would necessitate acknowledging a vulnerability that thoroughly contradicts decades of meticulously maintained superiority.

She categorizes the aggressive turbulence within her digestive tract as the entirely proper response of a refined palate encountering discount botanicals. The bitter aftertaste lingering upon her tongue is identified not as a toxic anomaly, but rather as the hallmark of a deeply offensive harvest originating from an utterly disreputable merchant. Her refusal to accept her medical reality operates as an imposing fortress against the profound indignity of physical collapse. She issues a formal demand that Beaumont officially record her grievance regarding the estate's suppliers, framing her ungraceful departure from the study as a righteous bodily protest against provincial hospitality.

By steadfastly maintaining this narrative of culinary insult, she preserves her position as the aggrieved party in a matter of strict etiquette. The sheer volume of her complaints regarding the substandard Earl Grey provides a convenient justification for her continued residence among the bed linens. Her insistence upon the tea's inferior quality ensures her confinement remains strictly classified as an exercise in extreme displeasure, an event demanding formal apologies rather than a physician. This rigid declaration serves as a proper dismissal, granting Beaumont the authorization to withdraw from her quarters and attend to the problematic spillage downstairs.
