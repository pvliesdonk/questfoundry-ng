Abandoning his deeply unsociable patrol of the second-floor corridor, Arthur descends the grand staircase with the shambling, imprecise gait of a gentleman who has recently converted his own bedchamber into an impromptu mortuary. Sleep deprivation has stripped him of his usual deferential posture, leaving a rigid, hollow-eyed accountant who firmly clasps his leather briefcase as a makeshift shield against the evening's cascading incivilities. Drifting toward the drawing room hearth, Arthur executes a remarkably subtle lateral shuffle, systematically closing the distance between his polished wingtips and a heavy brass fire poker resting upon the masonry.

To arm oneself with fireplace accoutrements during a formal gathering constitutes a staggering violation of the host’s hospitality. Nevertheless, Arthur allows his fingers to hover near the cool metal, unconsciously seeking a bludgeon to employ against his fellow attendees. His internal constitution is currently compromised by an agonizing string of private hypotheses regarding the status of his accounting ledger. Whether that damning volume has been reduced to untraceable ash, or whether its catastrophic contents have already been broadcast to the surviving guests, remains a matter of unresolved conjecture. He weighs the distinct possibility that his peers are presently navigating the parlor fully burdened by the knowledge of their impending financial ruin. Such an awareness would naturally transform the customary exchange of pleasantries into a theater of motive-laden hostility.

Arthur calculates the exact arc required to swing the brass implement, should a fellow guest attempt to address his bookkeeping methodologies. The mere contemplation of striking an acquaintance with a hearth tool represents an unforgivable lapse in decorum, equating to absolute social suicide, yet the environment strongly suggests that conventional protocols of dispute resolution are no longer applicable. Before he may firmly grasp the poker and finalize his descent into outright barbarism, a severe mechanical intrusion shatters the room's tense atmosphere.
