Having firmly established through rigorous geometric calculus that the primary provisions cupboard cannot accommodate a supplementary occupant without severely compromising the arrangement of the pickled onions, Beaumont addresses the secondary bio-hazard with decisive, albeit highly irregular, hospitality. He unlatches the nearest casement window, prioritizing the preservation of his immaculate patent leather oxfords as he leverages the freshly expired gentleman over the sill. The act of propelling an entire human form into the unforgiving winter night strains the deliberately narrow shoulder cut of his tailored velvet smoking jacket, prompting an immediate and necessary adjustment of his silk cuffs to reassert a modicum of personal control. He secures the glass partition with a definitive closure, quietly entertaining the professional hope that the sub-zero temperatures will serve as an adequate preservative for this exterior guest, especially when contrasted against the deeply regrettable state of the indoor arrival.

The prevailing meteorological hostility proves to be unexpectedly cooperative. A vigorous squall of heavy precipitation rapidly envelops the displaced gentleman, constructing a pristine, alabaster mound that temporarily resolves the aesthetic crisis. This external arrangement represents a vastly superior administrative solution when measured against the initial tragedy. The first casualty, currently sequestered amongst the larder shelves, continues to thaw at an entirely unacceptable ambient room temperature, threatening to deposit heavily pigmented bodily fluids across the domestic linoleum. The exterior snowbank, by contrast, demands absolutely no application of the meticulously folded emergency linen Beaumont currently harbors in his breast pocket.

However, the structural integrity of this frozen concealment proves distressingly brief. A sudden, violent updraft entirely reorganizes the snowbank, unearthing a singular, rigidly extended hand that grasps upward toward the glass with profound incivility. The unprompted visual introduction of a frozen human extremity into the evening’s ambiance registers as a conversational disruption of the absolute highest severity. The surviving assembly immediately relinquishes all pretense of formal posture. They execute an entirely unchoreographed migration away from the window, their rapid trajectory funneling them directly into the primary corridor. This unseemly retreat deposits the highly agitated collective perilously adjacent to the pantry door, which remains the solitary barrier shielding the company from the heavily compromised individual within. Beaumont adopts his widest lateral stance, locking his jaw and rigidly aligning his spine to physically barricade the hallway against the encroaching domestic chaos.
