title: Marren's Logbook
---

This is the sealed logbook drawn from the column of the forty-second lamp: waterlogged leather, greenish stains soaked deep, and a final page where the ink has never dried. It reads like a mother's voice, its warnings folded quietly inside its instructions, so that a careful reader learns two things at once. Its seal bears a plain closed ring crossed by a single line — the same mark Marren scored into the base of lamp forty-two. Along its margins runs a second, older hand in browner ink, leaning the wrong way, answering her notes rather than instructing, as though the book had been kept in company across long years. The last page is a map, still wet, of something vast circling beneath the canals. What it depicts, and whether it was ever meant to be finished, remain matters no one living can settle.
