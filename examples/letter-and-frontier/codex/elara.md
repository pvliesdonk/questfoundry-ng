title: Elara Windrider
---

Elara Windrider moves through the realm with the unadorned certainty of a courier, her travel‑worn cloak bearing the dust of countless roadways. Tucked hair frames quick eyes that scan horizons as readily as they scan the faces of strangers. Trained to bear messages without question, she nonetheless carries a quiet questioning beneath a stoic exterior; the weight of the sealed letter presses against her sense of duty. The queen’s envoy entrusted her with a parcel whose contents remain unseen, leaving her to balance obedience with the uneasy whisper of conscience. In every mile she walks, the tension between her autonomy and the role imposed upon her tightens, a thread of fragile hope that the peace she bears may hold the realm together.
