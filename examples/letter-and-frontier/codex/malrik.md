title: Malrik Stonehand
---

Malrik Stonehand stands at the iron‑bound gateway of Stonegate Fort, his broad shoulders capped by a scar that maps a past conflict across his left cheek. Clad in armor edged with cold steel, he embodies the fortress’s relentless order, his gruff voice tempered by a fairness that few can dispute. As gatewarden, he supervises the flow of travelers and the verification of sealed documents, his decisions often guided more by practicality than sentiment. Though his demeanor is unyielding, a subtle vigilance lies beneath, watching for any breach that might unsettle the fragile peace threading through the southern road. His presence offers both reassurance and the quiet pressure of unspoken scrutiny.
