title: River Clan
---

The River Clan rides the western waterways with a fluid grace, their nomadic riders bound by a code of exchange that blends protection with information. Masters of hidden gullied tracks across the silver‑strewn plain, they offer passage to those who honor their trust, while their caravans trade whispers as valuable as any coin. Their banners flutter beside the river’s edge, a signal that safety may be bought for a future favor. Though elusive, the clan’s reputation for shielding travelers from bandits is well‑known, and their ever‑watchful eyes record the movements of those who cross their domain. To journey with the River Clan is to walk a path where discretion and mutual obligation intertwine.
