title: Sealed Letter of the Dying Queen
---

The sealed letter of the dying queen bears a crimson wax seal that glints like fresh blood in the light, its integrity unmarred since its pressing. Beneath the wax lies an etched map, its lines hidden from view until the seal is broken, hinting at routes long forgotten. Within the parchment, a fragile peace proposal is written in trembling script, each phrase carefully weighed to bridge the chasm between warring courts. The seal’s material—pure beeswax—ensures that any tampering would be evident, a safeguard for the queen’s intent. Though the contents remain unreadable to all but those who dare disrupt the wax, the letter carries the weight of a realm’s hope, a promise that even in death, a sovereign may still steer the tides of conflict.
