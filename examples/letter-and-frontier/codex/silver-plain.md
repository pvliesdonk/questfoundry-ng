title: Silver Plain
---

The Silver Plain stretches endless beneath a relentless wind, a windswept steppe where the bones of forgotten caravans lie half‑buried in pale dust. Merchants and nomads alike brave its open expanse, the ground glittering like shattered glass under the sun’s harsh gaze. The plain’s reputation is both a promise of swift passage and a threat of exposure; nowhere offers the shelter of walls, and wandering eyes track every lone traveler. Occasionally, the River Clan’s riders slip through the gullies, offering covert protection to those who barter for their aid. Yet the plain remains a place where the thin line between safety and peril is drawn by the thinness of one’s own resolve.
