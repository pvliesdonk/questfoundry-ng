title: Stonegate Fort
---

Stonegate Fort dominates the southern road with its stone‑clad walls, a bastion of law that watches each traveler with an unblinking eye. Its garrison records every footfall, the rhythm of boots echoing through corridors where iron‑edged armor glints in torchlight. The fort’s gatewarden, Malrik Stonehand, enforces a strict order that prizes practicality over sentiment, ensuring that sealed missives are examined and, when necessary, validated by official stamp. Within its confines, the balance of power shifts with each petition, and the fort’s surveillance lends both reassurance and a palpable pressure upon those who seek passage. To cross the southern road is to submit to the quiet judgment of Stonegate’s unyielding vigilance.
