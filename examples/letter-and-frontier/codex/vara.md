---

Vara Shadowmantle moves within the rival court’s vaulted chambers like a breath of incense, silvery hair spilling beneath a half‑mask that conceals as much as it reveals. Known as the court’s spymaster, she measures every word, her soft‑spoken calculations whispering through corridors long before her presence is felt. Her keen eye discerns the slightest tremor in diplomacy, and she is famed for her willingness to bend a seal when advantage beckons. Though she operates in shadows, her motives are starkly pragmatic: to extract leverage from any diplomatic overture, however fragile. In the delicate dance of peace offers, Vara’s presence turns every sealed pact into a potential instrument of her court’s ascendancy.
