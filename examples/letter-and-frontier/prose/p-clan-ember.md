A warm ember glowed on the low stone slab, its dull light chasing rust‑colored dust across the floor. The River Clan riders formed a loose circle around it, their spears catching the amber glint as they murmured the oath that bound them to protect the messenger. Elara pressed her palm to the ember, feeling the modest heat seep through the cloth of her cloak. The promise of protection hummed softly in the quiet dusk, and she sensed the weight of the sealed missive hidden beneath her breastplate. She recalled the folded map she had uncovered earlier, its inked trails promising a shortcut through the mountains. A resolve settled in her chest; she would not tempt fate with the hidden route. She drew the thin strip of parchment from her satchel and pressed the scarlet wax seal over it, sealing the map as if to close a secret door. Beyond the camp, the Silver Plain stretched under a muted sky, its scarred earth waiting for her footsteps. The ember flickered, its glow a quiet oath as the clan reaffirmed their promise to guard her across the plain.
