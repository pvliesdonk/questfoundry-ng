title: Marek Sable, Chartmaker of the Western Shoals
---

Marek Sable moves like a tide across the battered decks of his vessel, a lanky figure swathed in an ink-stained coat that bears the ghosts of maps never finished. Storm-cloud eyes flicker with a restless curiosity, ever seeking the jagged teeth of reefs that have swallowed many a proud ship. Though his demeanor is earnest, a careful discipline underlies every line he draws; he measures not only latitude but the weight of hope he carries for those yearning beyond Greyrock's mist. Whispers among the fishermen speak of his willingness to barter passage for a piece of his cartography, yet no soul can claim to have truly read the depths of his ambitions.
