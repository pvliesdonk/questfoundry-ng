title: The Coastal Authority
---

The Coastal Authority is the bureaucratic spine of the region's maritime lifelines, its ironhanded pragmatism tempered only by the endless demand for the beacons that spear through the perpetual mist. Periodic supply ships slice through the grey seas, bearing ledgers, rations, and the weight of tribute demanded from isolated keepers like Elara. Their agents record every change in lighthouse fire, every deviation in cartographic surveys, and enforce a ledger of obedience that stretches from the mainland ports to the most remote sea stacks. While their presence ensures a semblance of order, the ever‑present paperwork cloaks a vigilance that watches for any sign of the ocean's deeper, quieter threats.
