title: Elara Wren, Keeper of the Greyrock Light
---

Elara Wren has tended the lighthouse flame longer than any living memory can recall, her weather-worn skin etched by sea‑spray and relentless wind. Grey hair, always bound in a tight braid, frames a face that speaks little but observes much, her eyes reflecting the dim, steady glow she sustains night after night. Methodical to a fault, she logs each tempest, each ship's silhouette, and each subtle change in the fog that presses against the stone. The lighthouse is both her charge and her prison, its looming presence a reminder of an oath older than the cliffs themselves, binding her to a duty as unyielding as the rock upon which it stands.
