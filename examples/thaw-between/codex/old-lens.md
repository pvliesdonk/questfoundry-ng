title: The Great Lens, Vessel of Light
---

Forged from sea‑glass that has weathered countless storms, the Great Lens sits at the heart of the lighthouse, its surface etched with ancient runes that shimmer faintly in the flame's glow. Though massive and seemingly immovable, its weight is a burden carried by the stone tower itself, as if the glass were a repository for something far older than the lighthouse's foundation. Legends among mariners whisper that the lens contains a fragment of the sea's dreaming power, a muted echo of the deep that once sang to the world. Its pristine condition, unmarred by salt or time, hints at a purpose beyond mere illumination, casting shadows that hint at secrets still submerged beneath the waves.
