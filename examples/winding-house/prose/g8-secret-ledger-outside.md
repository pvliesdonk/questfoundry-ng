The seam gives under your wire. The wire pin slides from your curls to shim the slipped plate and reveal a narrow cubby with a ledger bound in oilskin, edges rubbed thin and smelling of lampblack.

Your bones know a hiding place. You ease it out, the paper crisp as mica, columns ruled with a surveyor’s hand, households named in compact lines, hours tallied in red grains, arrows leading inward toward the mainspring, totals that add not to safety but to hunger.

Mira anchors a page. Her name sits inside a careful circle, ink dried to dry‑bruised brown, numbers added with margins neat, and you feel the borrowed lark chit in your apron tug as if tied to that ring, each hour you handed over pulling back through the paper and scraping a little shine off your recall.

The saving is a taking. Calder’s calculus weighs like a lead weight in a pocket, precise as teeth that only mesh because someone filed others down, and you taste iron where your breath catches on the fifth tick.

The hatch coughs dust. Rook climbs from the throat of the maintenance shaft with his map‑stitched coat creasing, eyes gone pale as chalked sky, and in two steps he is beside you, steady as slack tide, his fingers already thumbing the latch of a higher panel you had not seen.

He draws it open a finger’s width. Beyond is a slice of air with no pulse, a stillness pressed close as wool, stone silent enough that his tuning fork hums without sound, and the cold that breathes through it smooths the sweat from your lip and slows your count to a wider gait.

He speaks small. Let it slow, he says, share weight with what waits outside, let your city spin down to a survivable drift and learn a truer measure that is not bought with other people’s mornings, and he looks not at you but at the ledger as if hearing a worn bearing cry.

You hold the Key. Its shaft warms under your palm, pins set to catch with a snug promise if you turn them in, and the hatch’s dark edge presses along your knuckles like a rule laid straight to test if you will cut or soften the tooth.
